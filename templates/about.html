About the Project

Project Overview

The Global Inflation Forecast tool provides accurate inflation predictions based on historical data and advanced time series modeling. Our application allows users to examine inflation trends, analyze category-specific rates, and understand the impact of major global events.

Key Features

  • Forecasting: Predict future inflation rates with confidence intervals
  • Event Impact Analysis: See how major global events influenced inflation
  • Category Breakdown: Analyze inflation by specific economic sectors
  • Interactive Visualization: Explore trends through intuitive charts

Methodology

Our forecasting methodology combines statistical rigor with practical insights to provide reliable inflation projections.

Technical Approach

  • SARIMAX Modeling: We use Seasonal AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average with eXogenous factors (SARIMAX) models to capture complex patterns in inflation data.
  • Data Sources: Historical inflation data from trusted statistical agencies, updated regularly to ensure accuracy.
  • Confidence Intervals: All forecasts include confidence bands to represent the degree of uncertainty.
  • Event Correlation: Major economic events are mapped to inflation trends to analyze causative relationships.

The model is periodically retrained with the latest data to maintain accuracy. Forecasts are generated up to 10 years ahead, though accuracy diminishes with longer forecast horizons.
